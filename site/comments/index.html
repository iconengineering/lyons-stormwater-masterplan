Welcome to the Community Comment Map

This is the community comment map for the Town of Lyons Stormwater Masterplan.

One very important note about the data displayed on this map: the 100-year inundation areas and associated at-risk structures are not representative or derived from the FEMA regulatory 100-year floodplain.

The data on this map represents flooding from localized runoff and in no way affects any property owner's regulatory floodplain status.
